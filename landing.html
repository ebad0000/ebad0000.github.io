All you need to know

Lighting

Lighting is the most essential part of photography and videography as well as cinematography. Lighting can be natural or artificial based on your needs and the resources you have at your disposal. However, it is best practice to have some sort of strobe lighting in your bag when going for a professional shoot whether it is outdoors or indoors.

Composition

Composition is the deciding factor of a picture. You could have the best lighting and equipment and still not be able to produce a good picture and the only reason for that is because you donot know how to compose a shot.Composition is basically the idea of what to include in your frame and how to place your subject to bring out it's significance in a picture.

Equipment

Equipment is the baseline of your photography journey. Based on your needs you can combine several different lenses and lights to get your desired result.It is important to remember that your lens Decides the type of picture you take and your camera body decides the quality of the picture. So it is best to find the sweet spot in between to match your lenses and camera.

Editing

Editing is the final tool to process your image. The image your camera produces lacks the colors and lighting that you intend to express.Editing is the tool that lets you bring out the colors in an image.
